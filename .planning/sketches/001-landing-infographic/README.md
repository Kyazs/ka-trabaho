# Sketch 001: Landing Page Infographic

## Design Question
The current app immediately shows the AI matching form on load. This sketch explores three different approaches to creating an engaging landing page that builds trust and context before asking users to interact with the main features.

## How to View
Open: `.planning/sketches/001-landing-infographic/index.html`

Use the Phone/Tablet/Desktop buttons in the bottom-right toolbar to preview at different viewports.

## Variants

### A: Card Stats + Hero Illustration
- Hero section with badge, headline, and description
- Two CTA buttons: primary (Start AI Assessment) and secondary (Talk to Counselor)
- 2x2 grid of stat cards showing key numbers (300+ courses, ₱160 allowance, NC II cert, 95% placement)
- Three feature cards explaining the "How It Works" process
- Trust badges at the bottom
- **Best for:** Users who want to see social proof and stats quickly

### B: Step-by-Step Journey
- Hero focuses on "Your Path to Success" narrative
- Vertical step cards (1-4) showing the journey from current state to certified job
- Visual connector line between steps
- Stats + multiple action buttons at bottom
- **Best for:** Story-driven engagement, motivating users who feel lost

### C: Dashboard-Style Stats Grid
- Prominent dashboard header with data focus
- 3-column stat grid (Courses, Regions, Allowance)
- Feature cards with colored left borders for visual hierarchy
- Dual-action button layout (primary + two secondary side-by-side)
- **Best for:** Users who want to see concrete, structured data upfront

## What to Look For
- Which variant feels most trustworthy for a government vocational program?
- Which CTA placement makes you most likely to tap "Start AI Assessment"?
- Does the infographic content feel informative or overwhelming on a phone screen?
- Are the stats believable and compelling for a 15-24 year old audience?
